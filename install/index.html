▶ Open the app first (internal)
1

Make sure the app is open in Safari

Apple only allows Add-to-Home-Screen from Safari (not Chrome, WhatsApp, etc.). If you tapped the link in another app, tap Share → "Open in Safari" first.

2

Tap the Share button

The square icon with an arrow pointing up — at the bottom of Safari (or top right on iPad).

↗ Share icon
3

Scroll down → "Add to Home Screen"

You may need to scroll past the AirDrop / Messages row to find it.

4

Tap "Add" (top right)

The "NV Tracker" icon appears on your home screen — opens fullscreen, just like a native app. Works offline after first open.

5

Done!

Tap the icon to launch. PPTX export uses the iOS share sheet — pick Save to Files to keep on iPhone, or share to Mail / Teams.

⬇ Download APK (v3.0)
1

Tap "Download APK"

Above. The file is nvaudit-3.0-debug.apk — hosted directly on the portal.

2

Allow install from this source

First time only — Android will ask: Settings → Allow from this source. Normal for in-house apps not on the Play Store.

3

Tap the downloaded APK

From your browser's download notification, or open Files → Downloads and tap the .apk. Hit Install.

4

You're done

The "NV Audit" icon appears in your launcher. Native app with system share sheet, camera, and offline storage.

+

Or install as PWA (no APK download)

Open the web app in Chrome → tap ⋮ → "Install app". Lighter install, no permissions prompt, but share sheet is web-style instead of native.
